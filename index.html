Bunker Bros

  • Project created for P3 of EECS494. Design and build a game from scratch. Bunker Bros is a cooperative survival game where two players are protecting their bunker entrance from the zombie onslaught. One person controls a drone from above supporting a remote-controlled robot on the ground as they work together to fight off endless waves of zombies. Activate five radio towers scattered across the map to call for help and outlast one last wave of zombies before the military arrives. I developed:
  • Almost all of the UI for both the player and manager. Enemy AI by using Unity's built-in NavMesh. Hover system for the manager Health Bars + Damage indications Turning on Radio Towers etc.
  • Technology: C#, Unity, Photoshop, Premiere Pro
